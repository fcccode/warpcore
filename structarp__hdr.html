Detailed Description

A representation of an ARP header; see RFC826.

This representation is limited to making requests for IPv4 over Ethernet, which is sufficient for warpcore.

Field Documentation

uint8_t arp_hdr::hln

Length of the hardware address.

Will always be ETHER_ADDR_LEN for Ethernet.

uint16_t arp_hdr::hrd

Format of the hardware address.

Will always be ARP_HRD_ETHER for Ethernet.

uint16_t arp_hdr::op

ARP operation. Either ARP_OP_REQUEST or ARP_OP_REPLY.

uint8_t arp_hdr::pln

Length of the protocol address.

Will always be IP_ADDR_LEN for IPv4 in warpcore.

uint16_t arp_hdr::pro

Format of the protocol address.

Will always be ETH_TYPE_IP for IPv4 in warpcore.
